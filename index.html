About IFDC

The International Fellowship of Data Curation (IFDC) is revolutionizing the process of acquiring high quality training data by harnessing the synergy of generative AI and human incentive. We are like a profit-sharing initiative, similar to survey websites that compensate users for completing surveys. Our mission is to provide companies lacking sufficient custom data with high-quality training data for their LLM needs.

At IFDC, we incentivize both our community and dedicated fellows to format generated data as well as create unique data by hand, combining AI-generated content with the creativity and finesse of the human touch. This approach represents the future of training data, where AI and human collaboration lead to more effective and precise language models.
